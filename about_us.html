Organization History

Bronx Family Network is among NYCs leading non for profit organization providing services and assisting homeless families obtain suitable permanent housing. Bronx Family Network has very recent beginnings but it has quickly risen to be one of the top shelters in the city. We pride ourselves in accommodating families and helping them feel safe during this difficult transition.

Mission

Our mission is to provide safe and welcoming temporary housing for families in crisis.

Vision

To provide great case management work in order to prepare our families for independent living and become great contributors to their community.
